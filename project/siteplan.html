Siteplan for Dentistry Website

Site Name

Hintz Family Dentistry: This is the name of my father-in-law's dentistry practice. The name is representative of the personal touch available at the dentistry.

Site Purpose

This site provides information for patients and potential patients of the practice to get information regarding the care and treatment options available, as well as the ability to contact the office.

Scenarios

  • What types of dental services does this practice offer for general oral care?
  • How can I schedule an appointment, and what are the available hours?
  • Does this dental office accept my insurance, and what are the payment options for uninsured patients?
