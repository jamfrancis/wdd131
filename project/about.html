ABOUT US

At Hintz Family Dentistry, we designed our offices with our patients in mind. By combining a calm atmosphere with modern technology, we have created a place where our patients receive the quality of dental care they need and deserve. The moment you walk in our front door, our team will make you feel comfortable, secure, and cared for.

Our team of dental professionals has undergone a series of continuing education courses to keep us up-to-date on the latest dental techniques and industry standards. At Hintz Family Dentistry, we give each patient personal, one-one-one attention to explain procedures, listen to concerns, and answer questions. Most of our staff is fluent in Spanish and English so we can help each individual patient with their own needs.

Hintz Family Dentistry strives to provide lifetime care because oral health is integral to overall health. Our philosophy is to provide care that will be in your best interest now and in the years to come so you have a smile that will last a lifetime.

OUR SERVICES

We strive to give you a beautiful smile as economically as possible. Our knowledgeable staff will research your insurance benefits and explain what your coverage entails. We make sure that you know how much your treatment will cost before it has begun. We ensure that you know why you are in need of the treatments we recommend.
